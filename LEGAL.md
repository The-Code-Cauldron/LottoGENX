# Legal Notice — LottoGENX

## Statistical Tool Only

LottoGENX is a statistical analysis and data visualisation tool. It analyses historical UK National Lottery draw data. It does not predict future lottery outcomes.

Every UK National Lottery draw is an independent random event. Past draw frequencies have no causal influence on future draws. The law of averages framework presented in this tool identifies historical patterns and deficits. It does not create future certainty or increase the probability of winning.

No representation is made, and none should be inferred, that any combination generated or analysed by this tool is more likely to win than any other.
